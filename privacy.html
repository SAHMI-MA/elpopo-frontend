01 Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you purchase a product, book a private session, submit a contact form, join an email list, download a resource, communicate with us, or use the website. This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number, if submitted
  • Payment details, processed securely by Stripe
  • Billing information
  • Booking details
  • Form responses
  • Website usage data
  • Device, browser, and IP information

We do not directly store full card details. Payment information is handled by Stripe.

02 How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Deliver purchased products
  • Confirm payments
  • Schedule private sessions
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Send product access links
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve the website and offers
  • Send relevant educational or promotional emails
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

03 Email Communication

If you submit your email address, purchase a product, or request access to a resource, we may send you emails related to your purchase, access, updates, or educational content. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link inside the email.

Transactional emails related to purchases, access, bookings, or support may still be sent when necessary.

Payments are processed through Stripe. Stripe may collect and process payment information according to its own privacy policy and security standards. We do not sell or rent your payment information.

05 Contact Forms

When you submit a form, we collect the information you provide so we can respond to your inquiry, review your application, or provide support. Please do not submit sensitive personal information unless necessary.

09 Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for business, legal, support, tax, accounting, or operational purposes. You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to any legal or business obligations requiring retention.

10 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request correction of inaccurate data

To make a request, contact us through the website contact form.
